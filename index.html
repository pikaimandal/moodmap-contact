Mood Map

On Mood Map, users can share their mood every 24 hours using just an emoji, completely for free. Optionally, if users want to earn some passive income in WLD, they can predict a country's mood outcome in the next 24 hours by entering the reward pool with just 1WLD, and earn more WLD tokens if the users' predictions are correct.

But the Mood Map app will always be free and super fun to share users' moods. Users can engage with the World map and click on any country to see the country's moods shared by other users on the World app.
